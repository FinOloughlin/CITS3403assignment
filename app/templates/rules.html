How to Create a Mad Lib

Create a less then 1000 character prompt, remove between 3 and 10 words and replace them with [description], as shown below, submit!

Ensure all prompts are child safe (e.g. no profanity)

Example:
Many moons ago, [noun] was a simple [job] who lived in the town of [place] he lived a good life until the [evil group] attacked! now he must find the [item] and place it in the [place] to save the world.
